— About —

Redefining African Sound

HKR Manuel is more than just an artist—he's a storyteller, a cultural bridge, and a sonic architect reshaping the landscape of modern Afrobeats. Born in Ghana and raised on a diverse palette of sounds, Manuel crafts music that transcends borders and speaks to the soul.

From the intimate confessions of "Palmwine" to the hard-hitting honesty of "She Don't Fuck Around," each track is a journey through raw emotion, cultural pride, and unapologetic authenticity. His debut album "Have You Seen This Man?" has captivated audiences worldwide, earning critical acclaim and millions of streams.
